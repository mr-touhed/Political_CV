Participation In Political Activities

From 1995 until now, I participated as an activist of the Student League in various party activities including the hartal called by the Bangladesh Awami League against the immoral actions of the BNP Jamaat coalition government during the hartal I was harassed and arrested by BNP, Jamaat Shibir workers and police.

I was harassed and arrested several times by the workers and police of the then BNP Jamaat coalition government for holding marches, meetings, and gatherings to protest the grenade attack on Bangladeshi, countrywoman Sheikh Hasina on 21st August 2004.

Since 1995, as a worker of the Bangladesh Student League, Awami Jubo League, and Awami League, I was harassed several times by Chhatra Dal and Chhatra Shibir.

From 2006 to 2008, I played an active role in the downfall of the illegal caretaker government, and as an active activist of Chhatra League, I played a role in organizing the party by implementing the ideals of Father of the Nation Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman and with immense trust and faith in Bangabandhu's daughter Sheikh Hasina and I am still doing it.
